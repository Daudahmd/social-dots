Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and process.

How quickly can we get started?

We can typically begin with a discovery call within 24-48 hours of your inquiry. Our full project kickoff usually happens within 1-2 weeks, depending on project scope and your availability.

Do you work with businesses outside of Canada?

While our expertise is specifically tailored for the Canadian market, we do work with international businesses looking to expand into Canada or those that can benefit from our strategic approach.

What's included in a free consultation?

Our free consultation includes a comprehensive assessment of your current digital strategy, identification of key opportunities, and a preliminary roadmap with actionable next steps. No obligation required.

How do you measure project success?

We establish clear KPIs and success metrics at the beginning of each project. These typically include ROI improvements, process efficiency gains, user engagement metrics, and specific business objectives unique to your organization.
